Benefits

As a member of WiCyS, you have access to all the resources and opportunities provided by your network. From community groups to career fairs, scholarships to speaking opportunities, and much much more – check out all the benefits below and see what’s waiting for you!

AMPLIFY YOUR NETWORK

  • Expand your perspective by connecting with other leaders across academia, industry, research and government
  • Take advantage of collaborative opportunities within the members-only portal, including Special Interest Groups and Slack channels
  • Cultivate relationships through special interest groups such as Military Spouses, Data Privacy, Research, Latinas, Neurodiversity, Law, Veterans and more
  • Broaden your horizons through WiCyS conference scholarships, faculty grants, Veteran and BIPOC Fellowship Awards
  • Connect with new peers in cohorts for the mentor/mentee program, multiple skills development training programs and more
  • Learn from the best at invite-only Leadership Summit Working Groups
  • Enjoy priority consideration for the WiCyS conference waitlist and various non-WiCyS scholarships
  • Enhance your experience by attend WiCyS virtual events at no additional cost

AMPLIFY YOUR PORTFOLIO

  • Take the next step in your WiCyS journey by applying for a leadership role in one of our committees. Rather get involved elsewhere? Apply for participation instead.
  • Strengthen your portfolio through an abundance of media and speaking opportunities
  • Embrace new skills by attending members-only training programs and cybersecurity competitions for students/professionals
  • Share your expertise at the invite-only Leadership Summit and join a working group
  • Orchestrate or participate in women in cybersecurity-focused market research
  • Get recognized for your commitment to diversity in cybersecurity
  • Apply for an exclusive college scholarship reserved for WiCyS student members – Learn more here
  • Request a Fortinet Network Security Expert (NSE) exam voucher (valued at $400) available to WiCyS members. Email info@wicys.org for information.

AMPLIFY YOUR CAREER

  • Step into your future by creating a profile and uploading your resume to WiCyS Job Board++
  • Get a pulse on the opportunities waiting for you through year-round access to WiCyS strategic partner job postings
  • Strengthen your search by attending WiCyS virtual career fairs at no additional cost
  • Expand your network through the mentor/mentee program
  • Show your skills at the WiCyS technical conference, which provides the opportunity to interview on the spot with show sponsors
  • Arm yourself with the skills to succeed at the WiCyS career village, which features resume writing, mock interviews and LinkedIn assistance
  • Enjoy access to WiCyS Members-Only News Bytes with additional job opportunities
  • Obtain hands-on experience by working with WiCyS strategic partners and conference sponsors on specialty projects
